Home page

Introduction

Here at Cline's Cottage, we have creations that enlighten and inspire. The paintings of A.K. Cline show her deep devotion to her faith and her eye of creativity that gives each painting life. The works of Jenniflower including jewelry, flower arranging, and stained glass are sure to dazzle. Matthew’s Works, including woodworking and custom frames, leatherworking, and essential oils bring a touch of old world charm back to the present.

Images for the Home page

A.K. Cline Fine Art

A.K. Cline was born in Grand Junction, Colorado and grew up in various places in Colorado and Utah. This garnered a love for the mountains, forests, deserts, and she never lacked a place to explore. At a young age, she would doodle and enjoy making up her own characters. As time went on, she developed a talent for art and art history, although she was her own worst critic. An art teacher she once had, liked to teach a lesson of detachment by going to students' works in progress and painting a big black streak across the canvas. She recalls that when he came to her, he told her she ruins her own artwork. Nearing her graduation from high school, she had a choice of paths, and instead of going off to the Navy, she decided to go to college to pursue her art after being convinced by her father, her self-proclaimed biggest fan. After spending a short time at Colorado Mesa University, she transferred to Wright State University in Dayton, Ohio where she majored in Art and Sculpture, with a minor in art history. She raduated with her Bachelors Degree in 2020. She’s married to her husband Matthew, and has a daughter, Alanna.

Matthew's Works

Matthew Cline is husband to the renowned artist A.K. Cline. He grew up in Arcanum, Ohio and graduated from the local high school in 2010. Since then he has explored a couple different collegiate paths until settling on Applied Tech in the IT industry at BYU-Idaho. He has been dabbling in a number of different hobbies for most of his adult life. He has been deeply engaged with woodworking for the past couple of years, gaining enough knowledge to build his mother-in-law a bed frame, along with some smaller projects. He is beginning to learn about leatherworking and blacksmithing. He one day hopes to learn from his wife’s uncle, Jeremy Hodges, who is a manager of a leather working shop in Colorado. Matthew also plans to have an extensive garden with his wife at their new property, where they can grow fresh fruits and vegetables to feed their family, sell the excess, and distill many of the flowers, herbs, and other products into essential oils. Matthew also has crochet experience and has most recently made a baby blanket for his daughter! Lastly, he plans to frame each one of his wife’s paintings with custom frames, made right in the home shop. A.K. Cline worked as a professional custom framer for nearly a decade and has done museum-quality work countless times. With their knowledge combined, these two are an unstoppable creative force!

Jenniflower's Creations

Jenniflower is the mother of A.K. Cline and an enthusiastic lover of all things nature. Early in her adult life, she was the head of a small landscaping company in Colorado. Later, she showed her talent for flower arranging, working as a freelancer and donating much of her time and effort to memorial ceremonies in the Salt Lake area of Utah. She’s always had a creative mind, making small trinkets and jewelry from found materials in her spare time. Her proudest creations are her stained glass panels, whether in the form of windows, wall hangings, or garden sculptures.
